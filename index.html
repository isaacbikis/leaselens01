Real output — generated from a tenant's actual lease flags

Re: Lease clause amendments — [Your Address]


Hi [Landlord name],


Thank you for sending over the lease. I've reviewed it carefully and have a few amendments I'd like to discuss before signing. I believe these are reasonable asks and I'm happy to talk through any of them.


1. Entry notice (Section 9.2): The current language allows entry without advance notice. Colorado law (C.R.S. § 38-12-1203) requires landlords to provide at least 24 hours written notice except in emergencies. I'd like to update Section 9.2 to reflect this. Suggested language: "Landlord shall provide Tenant no less than 24 hours advance written notice before entering the premises, except in cases of emergency..."
